Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, list or purchase books, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)

  • Communication records

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services

  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users to facilitate transactions
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • When required by law or to protect rights

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Update your account information at any time
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your account

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes through our website or email.

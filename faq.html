Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list a book for sale?

To list a book, click on "Sell Books" in the navigation menu and fill out the book details form. You'll need to provide:

  • Book title and author
  • ISBN (optional but recommended)
  • Condition (New, Like New, Good, Fair)
  • Your asking price
  • At least one clear photo of the book

Once submitted, your book will be visible to potential buyers within minutes.

How does payment work?

BookSwap facilitates peer-to-peer transactions. When a buyer is interested in your book:

  • You'll receive a notification via email or in your account
  • You can arrange payment directly with the buyer
  • We recommend using secure payment methods like UPI or meeting in person on campus for cash transactions
  • For your safety, avoid sharing personal banking details

What condition should my book be in to sell?

We accept books in various conditions, but they must be:

  • New: Never used, still in original packaging
  • Like New: Gently used, no visible wear
  • Good: Some wear but all pages intact
  • Fair: Visible wear but still usable

Books with missing pages, excessive highlighting, or water damage cannot be listed.

How do I contact a seller?

To contact a seller:

  • Navigate to the book you're interested in
  • Click the "Contact Seller" button
  • Send your message through our secure messaging system
  • The seller will respond directly to your email or through our platform

For your safety, all initial communications should stay within the BookSwap platform.

What fees does BookSwap charge?

BookSwap is completely free to use! We don't charge any:

  • Listing fees
  • Commission on sales
  • Membership fees

Our mission is to help students save money on textbooks by connecting buyers and sellers directly.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your BookSwap account:

  • Go to your Account Settings
  • Scroll to the "Delete Account" section
  • Follow the prompts to confirm deletion

Note: This action is permanent and will remove all your listings and account information from our system.
